About

Hello! I'm Haegen, a 20-year-old Computer Science student at Institut Teknologi Bandung (ITB), Indonesia. With a strong passion for technology and innovation, I specialize in frontend - backend development as well as data science and data analysis, and have hands-on experience in web development, mobile app development, and machine learning. This personal website serves as a showcase of my technical skills, personal projects, and professional portfolio.

These projects reflect my ability to solve complex problems, work collaboratively in teams, and deliver high-quality solutions.

In addition to my technical skills, I am proficient in communication, problem solving, project management, team work, leadership, and have gained practical experience through being a web developer at SPARTA HMIF, and as a back-end developer intern at Mckinsey & Co. I am always eager to learn new technologies and take on challenges that push me to grow both personally and professionally.
